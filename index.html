FAQ

Is it safe for health-related services?

Yes. We add strict rules: no medical diagnosis, no promises. The agent focuses on qualification and logistics.

Do I need a new WhatsApp number?

Usually no. We connect to your business number.

What exactly is saved in Notion?

Client contact, answers to questions, notes, and status (new/qualified/paid).

Can a human take over the chat?

Yes — as an upgrade. MVP can run fully automated or with simple escalation.

Can you guarantee results?

No guarantees. We optimize the script, but conversions depend on offer, pricing, and demand.
